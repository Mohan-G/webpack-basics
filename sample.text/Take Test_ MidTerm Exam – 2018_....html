        MidTerm Exam
        Instructions

        Answer the following three questions in essay format. The response to each question should be at least 250 words in length. You need to include at least one source (both as a reference and as in-text citations) for each response.

        You will have 120 minutes to complete the exam after you have started it (in one sitting). Your exam will be automatically submitted when the 120 minutes expires or when the week comes to an end.

        Each question is worth 25 points (total point value of exam is 75 points). You will be graded on the originality and accuracy of your response, as well as your writing.

        This exam is to be completed individually by each student and not collaboratively. If there is any indication of students working together on any portion of the exam, all these students will automatically receive a zero score. Likewise, if there is any indication that a student shared information about this exam to other students, all these students will automatically receive a zero score.
        Timed Test This test has a time limit of 2 hours.This test will save and submit automatically when the time expires.
        Warnings appear when half the time, 5 minutes, 1 minute, and 30 seconds remain.
        Multiple Attempts Not allowed. This test can only be taken once.
        Force Completion Once started, this test must be completed in one sitting. Do not leave the test before clicking Save and Submit.

        Remaining Time:

         07 minutes, 39 seconds.
